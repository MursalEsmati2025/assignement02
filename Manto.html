Manto

Manto is one of the most popular Afghan food. it is an Afghan ravioli filled with beef

Ingredients

  • Minced beef, 300 gr
  • Onion, two medium-size
  • Ginger garlic paste, one teaspoon
  • Red chili powder, one teaspoon
  • Coriander powder, two teaspoons

Step-by-Step Cooking Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Make the Filling
  3. Roll and Fill Dumplings
  4. Steam the Manto
  5. Make the Yogurt Sauce
  6. Make the Split Pea Sauce
  7. Serve
