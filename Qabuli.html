Qabuli

Afghan Qabuli (Kabuli Pulao) is a fragrant rice dish topped with tender meat, sweet carrots, and raisins.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Lamb or beef
  • Carrots
  • Raisins
  • Onions
  • Spices (cumin, cardamom, cinnamon)
